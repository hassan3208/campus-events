About Our University

Welcome to University of Central Punjab (UCP)! Our university is one of the leading institutions in Pakistan, known for its academic excellence, modern facilities, and vibrant campus life. It is a place where learning meets fun and innovation.

Location

University of Central Punjab (UCP) is located in Johar Town, Lahore, Pakistan. The campus is easily accessible by public transport and is close to major landmarks of the city.

Why Visit Us?

Our campus hosts exciting events throughout the year including festivals, sports competitions, tech fairs, and cultural shows. There is always something happening here for everyone!
